2016-09-04 · 90 words · 1 minute read
General
FlexNetwork · HP · ASE

FlexNetwork Integrator This certification validates that you can deploy complex, unified enterprise-level campus networks in traditional, Cloud and Converged Infrastructure environments. Achieving this certification validates you can: Use open, standards-based solutions to support bandwidth-intensive, delay-sensitive, server-to-server traffic Prepare the network for innovations in cloud services, security, OpenFlow and BYOD (Bring Your Own Device) Integrate networking elements as well as servers and storage network-related components This certification also validates your expertise in one of three electives.
